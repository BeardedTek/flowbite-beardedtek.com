Rollover IT Services Agreement

The Bearded Tek

This Rollover IT Services Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into as of

by and between:

The Bearded Tek (“Service Provider”)

and

(“Client”)

Monthly Service Selection

(Same tiers as the public monthly-contracts flyer; edit amounts before printing.)

1. Services Provided

Service Provider agrees to provide the categories of service Client selects in the Monthly Service Selection. The selection summary below lists each chosen line with its monthly fee and, for Rollover tiers, the additional labor rate per hour. The following subsection describes scope for the selected service categories.

2. Business Hours

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, work is generally performed during normal business hours. After-hours or emergency work may be billed at different rates if stated in this Agreement or confirmed in writing.

3. Monthly Service Plan

Client agrees to pay recurring monthly fees for each service line Client selects in the Monthly Service Selection above, at the rates shown there (including amounts Client enters in the price fields), unless a different amount is agreed in writing before the Effective Date. A Rollover selection establishes prepaid support hours and the extra-hour labor rates shown for that tier. Backups and Monitoring selections establish covered device counts or endpoint tiers for those services. An All Inclusive selection covers the bundled combination shown for that tier; bundled components are priced together unless Service Provider agrees otherwise in writing.

4. Rollover Policy

Unused Monthly Hours will roll over for up to three (3) consecutive months. Hours older than three (3) months will expire and be removed from the Client's balance. The maximum accumulated balance cannot exceed three (3) months of unused hours unless otherwise agreed in writing. Hours are deducted from the oldest available balance first.

5. Backups, monitoring, and All Inclusive

The categories in section 1 and the Monthly Contract detail pages on beardedtek.com referenced in section 1 control what is included when Client selects Backups, Monitoring, or All Inclusive in the Monthly Service Selection. Those pages are incorporated by reference as they read on the Effective Date. If the Monthly Service Selection, this Agreement, and those detail pages ever conflict on scope, the signed Agreement controls. Only categories Client selects in the Monthly Service Selection are included under this Agreement unless added later in a signed writing.

6. Billing and Payment

Client authorizes automatic monthly billing via credit card. Billing will occur on the 15th of each month through Service Provider's invoicing system. Payments are processed securely via Stripe. Failure to process payment may result in suspension of services until payment is resolved.

7. Rates for Additional Services

Any Rollover services exceeding the available balance of hours will be billed at the additional labor rates shown for Client's selected Rollover tier in section 1 (and reflected in the selection summary), unless a different rate is stated in this Agreement or agreed in writing. For plans without a Rollover component, additional services are billed as agreed in writing. Additional charges will be invoiced separately and are due upon receipt unless otherwise agreed.

8. Scheduling and Response Time

Service Provider will make reasonable efforts to respond to requests in a timely manner. Response times may vary based on workload, priority, and service level agreements if applicable. Emergency or after-hours support may incur additional charges.

9. Term and Termination

This Agreement operates on a month-to-month basis. Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination: Any unused hours remaining within the valid rollover period may be used during the notice period. No refunds will be issued for unused hours.

10. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to: Provide necessary access to systems, equipment, and networks; maintain valid backups of critical data; ensure a safe working environment for on-site visits.

11. Limitation of Liability

Service Provider shall not be liable for: data loss, business interruption, or consequential damages; issues caused by third-party services, hardware failures, or pre-existing conditions. Client acknowledges that IT services involve inherent risks.

12. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of any sensitive or proprietary information obtained during the course of this Agreement.

13. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Alaska.

14. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings.

15. Acceptance

By signing below, both parties agree to the terms of this Agreement.
